Civic Digital Fellowship

Coding it Forward, the Harvard Open Data Project, and Harvard professor (and former Deputy CTO of the US) Nick Sinai are teaming up with the Census Bureau and the IRS to explore starting an elite public-sector summer technology and data fellowship for college students.

We're still working on making this fellowship a reality, but here's how we envision it: a cohort of students would spend this summer in DC working with government agencies, potentially including Census and the IRS, on new technology and data science projects. We're hoping to provide mentorship and funding as well.

We'll be looking for everyone from data scientists and software engineers to designers, product managers, and digital communicators. We hope to have technical and non-technical roles available. If you're passionate about using your skills to serve the public, you might be interested in this opportunity!
